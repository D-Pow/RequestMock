MockRequests

MockRequests

Source:
  • src/MockRequests.js, line 5
MockRequests will mock both `XMLHttpRequest` and `fetch` such that one single configure call, and the entire app is provided with mocks. URLs not configured will be unaffected and still trigger an async request as normal.
